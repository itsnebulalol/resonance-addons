YouTube Music

Stream and browse your YouTube Music library.

Install in Resonance
Getting your token:
1. Install Proxyman and enable HTTPS decryption for oauthaccountmanager.googleapis.com
2. Open the YouTube Music iOS app
3. Find the request to /v1/issuetoken
4. Copy the Authorization: Bearer header value
